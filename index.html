faq

Emperium offers over 50 features including quality-of-life improvements, avatar enhancements, world utilities, and various advanced modifications.

Emperium started off as a project made out of spite and to prove a point, then as we continued updating it we decided to keep it as a free project.

Installation is straightforward: download BepInEx, extract it to your VRChat folder, download Emperium, place the dlls in the plugins file and the Emperium folder in the VRChat folder, and launch the game. Check our guide page for detailed step-by-step instructions.

Join our Discord server for support! Our community and developers are active and ready to help with any issues, questions, or feature requests you might have.

Yes Emperium is safe to use. However, using any third-party modifications carries risks.
